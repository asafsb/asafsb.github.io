Acme Energy currently has 2 data centers, one in Los Angeles and one in Long Beach. Most of the hardware is EOS/EOL and they would like to build out two new DCs that are farther away from one another. They want to keep the existing facility in L.A., build the second one in Dallas, and decommission the one in Long Beach within 6 months.
